Who we are

Your family's
dental home — for life

Every child deserves a dentist who makes them feel brave. Every parent deserves a practice that treats their family like their own. That's the promise we made when we opened our doors in Sugar Land over sixteen years ago — and it's the promise we keep every single day.

We're more than a dental office. We're a place where anxious children become confident patients, where families return generation after generation, and where the community knows they'll always be welcomed with warmth, skill, and genuine care.

From a baby's very first tooth to a teenager's smile transformation, from families with special needs to patients who've never felt comfortable at a dentist — we meet you exactly where you are and walk alongside you.

Pediatric Dentistry
Gentle, joyful dental care tailored for children of every age — from first-tooth exams to teen cleanings. We make their early experiences with dentistry ones they'll actually look forward to.
02
Sedation Dentistry
For children who feel anxious, have special dental needs, or require extensive treatment — our safe sedation options ensure a calm, comfortable experience that parents trust completely.
03
Special Needs Care
Our team is specially trained in adaptive techniques for patients of all abilities. Every patient — whatever their needs — deserves compassionate, dignified, expert dental care.
04
Dental Implants
Permanent, natural-looking tooth replacement that restores your smile and lets you eat, laugh, and live without a second thought. Expertly placed for results that last a lifetime.
05
Cosmetic Dentistry
Teeth whitening, veneers, bonding, and smile makeovers. Because a smile you're proud of changes everything — the way you walk into a room, the way you light it up.
06
Orthodontics & Invisalign
Braces and Invisalign for teens and adults. Straight teeth, properly aligned bites, and the quiet confidence that comes from loving what you see in the mirror.
07
Emergency Care
Toothache, broken tooth, lost crown — dental emergencies don't wait. Neither do we. Same-day emergency appointments available for your family when you need us most.
08
Preventive & General
Cleanings, X-rays, sealants, fluoride treatments, and comprehensive exams. The foundation of a healthy smile is built on consistent, expert preventive care — and we make it easy.

Hospital Dentistry
Our dentists hold privileges at Texas Children's Hospital — giving your child access to the highest level of specialized dental care, in the safest environment possible, when it matters most.

Meet your dental family

The faces behind
your family's care

Skilled, board-trained, and deeply human. Every member of our team is here because they love what they do — and the families they get to serve.

Dr. Hareeti Gill
Pediatric Dentist · Founder
DDS, NYU MS, UTHealth Pediatric Specialty Texas Children's Hospital

Originally from India, Dr. Gill earned her DDS from NYU and completed her pediatric residency and Master's at UTHealth Houston. She holds medical staff privileges at Texas Children's Hospital and is deeply committed to children with special needs — a passion shaped by years volunteering with the United Cerebral Palsy organization during dental school.

Dr. Emily Longwith
Orthodontist
DDS, UMKC MS Dentistry, Indiana BS Bio Engineering

Dr. Longwith earned her Bachelor of Science in Biological Engineering from the University of Missouri, her DDS from UMKC, and completed advanced orthodontic training and her Master of Science at Indiana University. She designs personalized treatment plans for children, teens, and adults — and believes a confident smile can truly change a life.

Dr. Vinu Sista
General Dentist
DDS, UCSF GPR, UTHealth Houston FAGD Fellowship

A 2005 graduate of UCSF Dental School, Dr. Sista completed her General Practice Residency at UTHealth Houston and recently earned her Fellowship through the Academy of General Dentistry. A lifelong learner, she also teaches Clinical Dentistry to the next generation at UTHealth School of Dentistry.

Dr. Gargi Mukherji
General Dentist
DDS, Texas A&M Hospital Residency Academic Educator

Dr. Mukherji graduated from Texas A&M University College of Dentistry (Baylor College of Dentistry) in Dallas and completed a one-year hospital residency at UTHealth School of Dentistry at Houston. She also serves as an educator in academia and loves caring for the oral health needs of her community.

Dr. Nishita Gohel
General Dentist
DMD, Boston University Dental Anxiety Specialist

Originally from Michigan, Dr. Gohel pursued her passion at Boston University Henry M. Goldman School of Dental Medicine. She is committed to helping patients achieve healthy smiles while easing dental anxiety — believing compassionate care is the key to building trust and a positive experience for every patient.

Dr. Matthew Olson
General Dentist
DMD, OHSU BA, Summa Cum Laude Fixed Prosthodontics

A Midwest native, Dr. Olson graduated summa cum laude from the University of North Dakota and earned his DMD from Oregon Health and Sciences University, where he received distinction in Fixed Prosthodontics. He is dedicated to individualized treatment planning, open communication, and continuing education.
